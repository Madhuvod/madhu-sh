This book will completely change your perspective on a lot of things. How knowledge works, what progress actually means, why some societies stagnate and others don't. Deutsch makes you rethink things you thought you already understood.

I felt genuinely dumb reading this. Lost count of how many times I had to go to Claude and ask what a specific word meant.

Great book. Just know what you're getting into.
